KAKE SOBA




Kake soba (かけそば), originally called "bukkake-soba," is a buckwheat noodle
dish made by pouring hot dashi based soba soup over boiled soba noodles.


HOW TO MAKE?


INGREDIENTS

2 portions of soba noodles, 500 ml (2 cups) dashi, 20 ml (4 tsp) dark soy sauce, 20 ml (4 tsp) light soy sauce,
50 ml (3 ½ tbsp) mirin, 5 ml (1 tsp) sake, 3 0g spring onion shoots (hosonegi) cut into 5cm pieces, and hichimi
togarashi (Japanese chili powder) to garnish

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Cook the soba noodles according to the packaging.
2. Add the dashi, dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, mirin and sake to a separate saucepan and bring to a boil.
3. Once boiling, lower to a simmer and add the spring onion. Allow to simmer for 2-3 minutes.
4. Divide the cooked soba into serving bowls and pour the broth over the top.
5. Place the cooked spring onion on top of each bowl.
6. Sprinkle shichimi (optional) or your choice of additional toppings.
