SUITON SOUP




Suiton Soup is hot soup with soft dumplings simply made from flour and water. Suiton is almost always eaten at home
not at restaurants. Our easy homemade Suiton with a lot of vegetables is so warm and filling. It is a perfect soup during
the cold seasons.


HOW TO MAKE?


INGREDIENTS

1/4 medium carrot, 1 inch daikon radish 2.5 cm, 3 inches gobo burdock root 7.5 cm, 1 white long onion,
1 chicken thigh, 2 1/2 cups 600 ml dashi, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp sake, 1/2 tsp salt

Suiton Dumpling

1/2 cup flour, 2 tbsp potato starch, 1 tbsp nagaimo long yam grated, and 1/2 cup water

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Peel carrot and cut in half vertically, then thinly slice into half rounds. Peel daikon and cut into quarters.
Slice thinly. Slice Gobo root diagonally thinly. Soak gobo in water for 5 minutes. Cut white long onion into 1/2‚Äù (1.2 cm)
length pieces. Cut chicken into small pieces.
2. In a medium pot, heat dashi to a boil and add chicken and all the vegetables except the green part of the onion.
Cook at medium heat until vegetables get tender, about 5 minutes. Then season with soy sauce, sake, and salt.
3. In a medium bowl, add all the ingredients for dumplings and whisk together well.
4. Drop spoonfuls of dumpling batter into the soup, and cook covered for 5 minutes. Add green parts of
onion at the end and stir. Serve immediately.
