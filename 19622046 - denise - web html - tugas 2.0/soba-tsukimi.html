TSUKIMI SOBA


Tsukimi soba (月見そば) is made by topping buckwheat soba noodles with an egg and chopped green onions. This soba includes
carrots, green onions, and a broth consisting dashi, soy sauce, sake, and mirin.


HOW TO MAKE?


INGREDIENTS

6 cups store-bought or homemade dashi soup, 1/3 cup soy sauce, 2 tablespoons mirin,
1/2 teaspoon salt, 14 ounces dried soba noodles, 4 large pasteurized eggs,
1/4 cup thinly sliced green onion

INSTRUCTIONS

1. In a medium pot on medium heat, add the dashi soup, soy sauce, mirin, and salt.
Cook, stirring occasionally, until the mixture begins to boil.
2. Meanwhile, prepare the soba noodles according to package directions. Drain well.
3. Bring the soup to a rolling boil. Divide the soba noodles between four bowls.
4. Carefully pour the hot soup over the noodles in each bowl.
5. Immediately crack an egg on top of the noodles in each bowl.
6. Divide the green onion between the bowls and serve immediately.
