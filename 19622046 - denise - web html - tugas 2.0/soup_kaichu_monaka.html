KAICHU MONAKA SOUP




Monaka (最中) is a Japanese sweet made of azuki bean paste sandwiched between two thin crisp wafers made
from mochi. The wafers can have the shape of a square, a triangle, or may be shaped like cherry
blossoms, chrysanthemums, and so on.


HOW TO MAKE?


INGREDIENTS

Monaka, hot/boiling water

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Peel some of the monaka skin until it shows the filling and put it into the bowl.
2. Pour some boiling water.
3. Stir and let it soak for a while.
4. Serve immediately.
