ICHIGONI SOUP




Ichigoni (いちご煮) is a local dish in Hashikami Town and Hachinohe City. This specialty food is actually
luxurious according to locals. In fact, they only use high-quality and expensive ingredients such as abalone soup
and sea urchin.


HOW TO MAKE?


INGREDIENTS

280 g dashi soup, 6 ml light soy sauce, 6 ml liquor, 2 g salt, 20 g abalone, 30 g sea urchin, 1 sheet perilla leaves

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Combine all wet ingredients and salt.
2. Cut the abalones into thin slices.
3. Toss the sea urchins into the pan and let it simmer in low heat.
4. Transfer into a bowl and top with chopped perilla leaf.
